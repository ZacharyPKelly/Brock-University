About

Fosebooks.com is a free online resource for the use of computational methods in science, mathematics and engineering. It features course content in the form of Jupyter Notebooks and Books. The creation of the site is supported by Academics Without Borders through a collaborative project between the University of Alberta and the University of Eswatini.

Contributors: Simiso Mkhonta, Sandile Motsa, Peter Berg, Phillipe Nadon, Joseph Menezes, Harshil Vyas, Zachary Kelly
